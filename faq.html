Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

We offer insurance for cars, two-wheelers, and commercial vehicles with customizable plans. Our comprehensive coverage includes protection against accidents, theft, natural disasters, and third-party liabilities.

We partner with trusted insurers, provide transparent policies, and ensure quick claims processing for your peace of mind. Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist you with any queries or concerns.

Claims Process

You can file a claim online via our portal or by visiting our nearest office with required documents. Our streamlined process ensures quick processing and minimal paperwork. You can also call our 24/7 helpline for immediate assistance.

Claim processing time varies depending on the complexity of the case. Simple claims are typically processed within 3-5 working days, while more complex cases may take up to 15 working days. Our team keeps you updated throughout the process.

Payment & Pricing

Yes, we provide flexible monthly, quarterly, and yearly payment options to suit your budget. You can choose the payment frequency that works best for you, with no additional charges for any payment plan.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, online payment platforms, and mobile wallets. Our team will provide you with all available options when you purchase your policy.
